Common 3D Printing Problems

Click a problem to see how to fix it.

1. Filament Clog
Heat nozzle, push filament, cold pull, clean nozzle.
2. Bed Adhesion Issues
Clean bed, level bed, adjust Z-offset, slow first layer.
3. Warping
Use heated bed, enclosure, brim/raft.
4. Layer Shifting
Tighten belts, stabilize printer, reduce speed.
5. Stringing
Increase retraction, lower temp.
6. Under-Extrusion
Check clogs, increase flow, free filament spool.
